THE SHEETS


RELEASED 7TH AUGUST 2020.


'The Sheets' was released as part of the bands first EP release with a record been printed by French label Beast Records. The whole EP was recorded with Alex Greaves at The Nave in Leeds."

A word from the artist: "I had had the lyrics for many months beforehand and they fitted perfectly with the space of the track. Well I will let you be the judge of that. The meaning was showing the dedication yet detriment that a relationship could cause. The spiraling sadness that follows a breakup, the juxtaposition between hatred and love and the adjustment away from that I suppose."

Clash Music -
"Musically, LUMER seem to play with pre-existing notions of space - the taut, claustrophobic arrangements are shot against the broader, more nuanced production."
Yuck Magazine -
"‘The Sheets’ unfolds the sadness of a broken heart and the juxtaposition between hatred and love. The track unveils the sinister side of restless romance."
